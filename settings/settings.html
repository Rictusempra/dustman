Dustman only closes tabs which have been in the background for this long.

Dustman closes tabs only in windows that have more than this many tabs. Pinned tabs are not counted towards this limit, and never auto-closed. While Tabs that play sound (but not pinned) are counted towards this limit, they are also never auto-closed.

Dustman keeps a list of pages that were opened in the tabs it closes. They can be accessed via the garbage bin button in the toolbar. This option controls how many pages are saved at most. If the limit is reached, the oldest pages are discarded.
